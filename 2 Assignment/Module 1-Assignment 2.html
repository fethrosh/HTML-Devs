Favorite Sport

My favorite sport, maybe unsurprisingly, is chess. However, my reason for liking this sport is not what one usually thinks about when thinking about someone who is interested in it. My main reason for liking the sport of chess is that I am able to keep it as a background for when I am writing a story. Don't get me wrong, chess is an interesting thing to watch, but I believe there is no way for me to do as well as the people playing the game. As such, it isn't something that I enjoy participating in, but rather something that I like watching happen. Although I feel like everyone knows the rule to chess, I will go over them here Certain pieces move certain ways: 
	The knight can move in an L shape.
	The pawn can only move forward 1 open space after it's first move, but up to 2 open spaces for it's first.
	The queen can move in an unlimited amount of open spaces diagonally or along the board.
	The bishop can move an unlimited amount of open spaces diagonally.
	The rook can move an unlimited amount of open spaces along the board
	The king can move only 1 open space in any direction.
	All pieces except the pawn can capture any piece in their way, but stop on that spot. The pawn captures diagonally 1 space.
