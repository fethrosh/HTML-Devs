Miscellaneous

Don't know how interesting this will be to whoever's reading this, but I am very interested in ConLangs. I don't know when I started being interested in them, but they've always been the closest thing to a complete creative process for me, since I have had very little opportunity to have a creative outlet other than stories. One of my favorite words from the languages that I have created is tevugarl, which means fish. Kinda a wierd thing to like about a language, I know, I know. On the other hand, there is nothing more important to someone who is a fisherman, so I guess it's a matter of perspective
